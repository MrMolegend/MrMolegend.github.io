Welcome to Oundle Angel Cars

We provide reliable and affordable taxi services in Oundle and surrounding areas. Whether you're looking for an airport transfer, a local trip, or a long-distance journey, we are here to serve you 24/7 with professional drivers and clean vehicles.

About Us

At Oundle Angel Cars, we pride ourselves on offering safe, comfortable, and efficient transportation services. Our fleet includes well-maintained cars and highly trained drivers who prioritize your safety and satisfaction.

We have been serving the community for over a decade, and our commitment to quality service remains unwavering.

We have our own dedicated workshop and employ the services of an Independent Vehicle Examiner. All Angel vehicles undergo a 4-weekly safety check and are registered either with East Northamptonshire District Council or the Eastern Area Traffic Authority.

Our Personnel We employ friendly, experienced drivers who undergo frequent medical examinations and have current 'enhanced' CRB (Criminal Record Bureau) disclosures in force..
